Trilha Trading Quantitativo

Asimov - Fundamentos de Trading Quantitativo

The "Foundations of Quantitative Trading" course is a comprehensive guide to mastering Quantitative Analysis using programming and Data Science in Financial Markets. Through a data-driven approach, students learn Python programming and essential tools like Pandas, enabling them to create and control their own investment strategies. The course covers key financial concepts, quantitative methods, and statistical foundations, equipping participants to analyze investment strategies based on data.

Backtesting – Avaliando Estratégias de Trading com Dados Históricos

This essential program is designed for traders and investors aiming to simulate their investment strategies using historical data, reducing risks by validating strategies before real market application. You'll learn the fundamentals of backtesting, how to implement simulations in Python, and evaluate strategy performance with key metrics like Drawdown and the Sharpe Ratio.
